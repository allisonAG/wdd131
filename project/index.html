What is Tales & Teacups?

Tales and Teacups is a monthly reading club designed for book lovers who enjoy sharing stories, ideas, and a warm cup of tea. Each month, members can choose from a curated list of book selections, read the book of the month, and join a cozy gathering to discuss the story, explore different perspectives, and connect with fellow readers.
It’s more than just reading — it’s about building community, enjoying meaningful conversations, and making new friends through a shared love of books and tea. Whether you're a lifelong reader or just getting started, you're welcome here.
